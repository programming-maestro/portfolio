Program overview

This program emphasized applying data science techniques to real-world business and engineering problems, with a strong focus on analytics, experimentation, and data-driven decision making.

  • Statistics, probability, and exploratory data analysis
  • Supervised and unsupervised machine learning fundamentals
  • Data modeling, feature engineering, and evaluation metrics
  • Experimentation, analytics validation, and insight generation

The learning directly supports my work in platform QA, analytics integrity, experimentation frameworks, and AI/ML quality initiatives across large-scale product ecosystems.
